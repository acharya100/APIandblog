Detailed Description

Virat Kohli was born on November 5, 1988 is one of cricket's greatest batsmen with a record 50 ODI centuries and over 8,000 IPL runs for Royal Challengers Bengaluru. He led India to the 2011 Cricket World Cup and 2013 ICC Champions Trophy victories earning the ICC ODI Player of the Year four times (2012, 2017, 2018, 2023). With 274 million Instagram followers, he ranks among global icons like Cristiano Ronaldo and Lionel Messi by charging Rs 12 crore per post. His Virat Kohli Foundation supports underprivileged children through charity events.
