Detailed Description

John Cena was born on April 23, 1977 is a 17-time WWE World Champion. He ties the record for most world title reigns. He transitioned to Hollywood with roles in films like Fast and Furious 9 and The Suicide Squad and webseries like Peacemaker which builded a global fanbase. With 22 million Instagram followers Cena engages fans with unique content though his social media earnings are less documented than Kohli or Messi. His 'You Can't See Me' catchphrase and Make-A-Wish foundations' contributions highlight his cultural impact.
